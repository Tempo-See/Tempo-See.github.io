熏 香

Incense

香是看不见的，
但它会让一个空间慢下来。

我把试过的香记在这里——
不为评判，
只为记得它们路过的样子。

闻香识季，
识人，
也识自己。

Incense is invisible,
but it slows the room.

I keep notes here on the ones I've burned—
not to judge,
just to remember how they passed through.

Read the season by scent,
read others, read yourself.
