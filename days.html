Days

三本日记，
记我走过的，
和我没有走远的。

有时是风吹过窗户，
有时是路上的某个下午，
有时是夜里忽然想起的一句话。

日子是慢的，
翻页也是。

Three notebooks —
one for places I've been,
one for places I haven't gone far from.

Sometimes the wind through a window.
Sometimes an afternoon on the road.
Sometimes a sentence that arrives at night.

Days move slowly —
so do the pages.
