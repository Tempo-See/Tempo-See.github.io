Photography

我喜欢拍那些差点错过的东西。
风刚停的那一秒，
光从一个屋子走到另一个屋子的途中。

没人会注意，
但它存在过。

不为定格，
只为「曾经看见」。

I photograph things almost missed.
The second after the wind stops,
light moving from one room to another.

No one notices,
but it was there.

Not to capture,
only to have seen.
