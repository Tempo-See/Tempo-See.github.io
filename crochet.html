III · Crochet

钩  针

Crochet

一根线，
从指尖开始，
慢慢长成一件东西。

我喜欢这种——
能用手摸到时间的事。

把日子打成结，
反而能松开心里的某一处。

A single thread,
growing from my fingertips,
slowly into something whole.

I love this kind of thing—
where time can be touched.

Knotting the days,
somehow loosens something inside.
