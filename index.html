节   奏

万物有自己的节拍——
潮汐有时，花开有时，呼吸亦然。

我把这里留给那些
让我慢下来的事。

不为陈列，只是经过。

All things keep their own time—
the tide, the bloom, the breath.

I leave this place
for the things that slow me down.

Not to display. Only to pass through.
