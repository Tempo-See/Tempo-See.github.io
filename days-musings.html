浮生漫旅 A Drifting Life

drifts
2025.04.21 i

今天什么也没做。
下午看着窗外，
云很慢地走过去。

忽然觉得自己也走得很慢，
但那不是一件坏事。

Did nothing today.
Watched the clouds drift past
through the afternoon window.

Realised I move slowly too —
and that isn't a bad thing.

—— 慢一点没关系。
—— it's okay to be slow.

2025.02.04 v

很多事的「意义」其实都是后来才有的，
当下只是在做。
当下只是「正在做」本身。

所以也不必太急，
也不必每一步都问问自己是不是值得。

只是先做，
然后日子会替我们解释。

The "meaning" of things often arrives later.
In the moment, you're just doing.
The moment is just "doing" itself.

So no need to rush —
no need to ask at every step whether it's worth it.

Just do it first.
The days will explain it for us, after.

—— 写于一个失眠的夜。
—— written on a sleepless night.
